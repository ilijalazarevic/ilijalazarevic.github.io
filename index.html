Turning ‘We Should Use AI’ Into Working Solutions

I bridge the gap between AI's promise and your business reality, with implementations that actually work.

Who I am

I have been on both sides of the table. I have worked in corporate and startup environments, both hands-on and as an engineering lead.

A few years ago, I built an AI-powered project. I wrote the code, hired the engineers, made the architecture calls, and learned firsthand what it takes to get AI from a good idea to a working product.

That experience taught me something most consultants never learn: the hardest part of AI isn't the technology. It's knowing where the technology actually fits.

When I sit down with a founder today, I bring all of that to the table. I've built the systems. I've made the mistakes. I know what works, what doesn't, and how to tell the difference quickly.

AI Opportunity Screening

A structured process to find where AI actually fits your business before you spend a cent on implementation.

For founders and decision-makers at small and mid-sized companies who see the potential of AI but don't have the time to cut through the noise.

1

Introductory Call

30 min · Free

2

Deep-Dive Interviews

2–3 hours · Remote
